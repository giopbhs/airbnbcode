Meet The Staff

The surgical staff at Dr. Gustave have the latest training in all the surgical procedures performed in our office. All surgical staff go through extensive training and are required to complete the Oral Maxillofacial Anesthesia Assistants Program conducted by our national association, the American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons. Our staff work in conjunction with the Doctor to provide you the safest and most efficient care possible. All surgical staff are CPR certified and are required to recertify every two to four years.
